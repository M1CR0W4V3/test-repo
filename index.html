Local Table Tennis Website

Welcome to the local table tennis website. We are open currently from 7:30pm - 10:00pm As members of the community and the surrounding areas such as; Apalachin, Binghamton, Endwell, Johnson City and Owego, we have established a Table Tennis Club (Ping-Pong) in the area.

For those who do not know a lot about table tennis, it is a lively and energetic paddle game to be enjoyed by all! It is our job as a club to bring quality equipment, facilities, and players together for the greater good and for the community. Similarly, we are looking to expand to the people in the area.

People who enjoy the sport or wish to learn are welcome to come and join the competition!
